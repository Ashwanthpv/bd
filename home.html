WELCOME TO BlueDiamond Water Solutions

Blue Diamond Water Solutions is a reputed service provider offering water treatment, installation of the water treatment plants and drinking water projects. Set up in the year 2009, today Blue Diamond Water Solutions, is the most trusted name for water treatment systems. Our commitment to quality, contemporary design, innovation, quick turnaround time and competitive pricing coupled with a ‘passion to excel’ has made us the preferred choice of OEMs and Corporate clientele. It is our attitude to ‘deliver the best’ that has made us one of the fastest growing companies, professionally managed with highly experienced manpower, state-of-the-art technology for production of water treatment plant, water treatment chemicals and specialty chemicals. With the encouragement and success, we tasted, we are planning to extend our range of services and products.
